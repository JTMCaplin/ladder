Privacy Policy

Last updated: 20 August 2019
ladder requests details about your team's users (real name, avatar, status) as well as information about your team's custom emoji.

ladder stores only the basic information required to create and maintain the leaderboard: your match outcomes and dates. No personal information is recorded; only team and user IDs are stored. Usernames in /ladder responses are generated by Slack, outside of ladder's view.

ladder will never sell your data, either in the aggregate or any data specific to your user(s).
